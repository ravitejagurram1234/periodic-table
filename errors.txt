Step 1 — remove the faults (PowerShell, repo root)

$path = "src\main\resources\wsdl\RequestService.wsdl"
$wsdl = Get-Content $path -Raw
$wsdl = [regex]::Replace($wsdl, '<wsdl:fault\s+name="QException".*?</wsdl:fault>', '', 'Singleline')
$full = (Resolve-Path $path).Path
[System.IO.File]::WriteAllText($full, $wsdl, (New-Object System.Text.UTF8Encoding($false)))
Write-Host "QException faults removed."

Step 2 — verify they're gone

Select-String -Path "src\main\resources\wsdl\RequestService.wsdl" -Pattern "wsdl:fault"
Expected: no output (zero matches). If anything prints, stop and tell me.

Step 3 — regenerate + compile

mvn clean compile
This re-runs codegen from the edited WSDL (now without the throws QException clauses) and compiles everything. Expect BUILD SUCCESS.
